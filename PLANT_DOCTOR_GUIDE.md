# Plant Doctor - User Guide

## Welcome to Plant Doctor! 🌿

Plant Doctor is your AI-powered botanical expert designed to help you identify plant diseases, learn effective cure methods, and maintain a thriving garden.

## Getting Started

### 1. **Enter Your Name**
Start by entering your name on the landing screen to personalize your plant care journey.

### 2. **Chat with Plant Doctor**

## How to Use Plant Doctor

### Example 1: Plant Disease
**You**: "My tomato leaves have brown spots with yellow halos"
**Plant Doctor**: Identifies early blight, provides treatment with fungicides, prevention tips, and organic alternatives

### Example 2: Pest Problem
**You**: "I see tiny white insects on my rose bush"
**Plant Doctor**: Identifies as aphids, suggests neem oil, insecticidal soap, or natural predators like ladybugs

### Example 3: Prevention
**You**: "How can I prevent powdery mildew?"
**Plant Doctor**: Provides comprehensive prevention strategies including air circulation, spacing, and watering methods

## Common Diseases Covered

- **Fungal**: Powdery mildew, leaf spot, rust, blight
- **Bacterial**: Bacterial leaf scorch, bacterial blight
- **Viral**: Viral mosaic, leaf curl virus
- **Environmental**: Wilting, chlorosis, nutrient deficiency

## Pest Types Covered

- Aphids, spider mites, mealybugs
- Scales, thrips, whiteflies
- Japanese beetles, caterpillars
- Nematodes, slugs, snails
- And many more!

## Tips for Best Results

3. **Ask Follow-ups** - Ask clarifying questions if needed
